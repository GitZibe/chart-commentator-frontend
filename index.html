Only trade the NY open (8:30–11:30 AM EST)

Setup Guide

Get Profex running in under 5 minutes. No coding needed.

1
Subscribe to TradingView Essential or higher
Go to tradingview.com/pricing and subscribe to Essential ($14.95/mo) or higher. You need this to unlock webhook alerts. The free plan does not support webhooks.
→ tradingview.com/pricing
2
Open your chart and create an alert
Open any chart on TradingView (NQ, ES, Gold, etc). Press Option+A (Mac) or Alt+A (Windows) to open the alert creation window. Set the condition to a price level you want to watch.
3
Add the webhook URL
In the alert creation window, click Notifications → check Webhook URL → paste this URL:
https://chart-commentator-backend-production.up.railway.app/webhook
4
Paste the message payload
Click on the Message field in the alert window. Delete everything in it and paste this exactly — do not change anything except YOUR_SECRET:
{"secret":"YOUR_SECRET","symbol":"{{ticker}}","interval":"{{interval}}","price":"{{close}}","message":"{{strategy.order.comment}}"}
Replace YOUR_SECRET with the password given to you by Profex.
5
Create the alert and you're done
Click Create. The next time price hits your level, Profex automatically receives the alert, analyzes the chart structure using Smart Money Concepts, and posts the commentary here in real time. No further setup needed.
